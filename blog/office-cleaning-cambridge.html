Cambridge's Commercial Landscape

Cambridge is not a single office market — it is several distinct commercial districts, each with its own character, tenant mix, and cleaning requirements. Understanding these neighborhoods is central to delivering services that actually fit the way each building operates.

  • Kendall Square & the Innovation District — Kendall Square has earned its reputation as one of the world's leading biotech and technology hubs. Companies like Moderna, Sanofi, Akamai, Google, and hundreds of smaller life sciences firms occupy millions of square feet of Class A office and lab space along Broadway, Main Street, Binney Street, and the CambridgeSide corridor. These are high-security, high-standard environments where cleaning crews must navigate badge access, sensitive equipment, and the expectations of world-class organizations.
  • Harvard Square & Brattle Street — Harvard Square is home to a thriving mix of university-affiliated offices, law firms, financial advisors, nonprofit headquarters, and boutique professional practices. The offices here tend to be client-facing, often occupying historic or architecturally distinctive buildings that require careful attention to detail — hardwood floors, antique fixtures, and reception areas that must always look impeccable.
  • MIT Area & East Cambridge — The blocks surrounding MIT and stretching into East Cambridge house a dense cluster of tech companies, robotics firms, AI startups, and university research administration offices. These workspaces frequently feature open floor plans, maker spaces, communal kitchens, and flexible layouts that change with the pace of the companies occupying them. Cleaning programs here need to be adaptive and responsive to shifting configurations.
  • Alewife & Fresh Pond — The Alewife corridor along Route 2 and Concord Avenue has become a growing commercial office node. Multi-tenant office parks, corporate back-office operations, and professional services firms occupy modern buildings with ample parking and easy highway access. These facilities often operate on traditional business hours and need reliable after-hours cleaning that is completed before the next business day begins.
  • Central Square & Inman Square — Central Square's commercial mix includes coworking spaces, creative agencies, tech startups, and nonprofit offices housed in converted industrial and mixed-use buildings. Inman Square adds smaller professional practices and community-oriented organizations. Both neighborhoods feature offices with character — exposed brick, narrow stairwells, shared building entrances — that require cleaning crews who can work efficiently in compact, nonstandard layouts.

Biotech & Life Sciences Offices

Cambridge's biotech corridor houses some of the most important pharmaceutical and life sciences companies in the world, and their office spaces often sit adjacent to controlled laboratory environments. While Blue Brick focuses on the office, common area, and administrative portions of these facilities, we understand the heightened standards that biotech companies expect. Our crews use HEPA-filtered vacuums, low-residue cleaning solutions, and systematic top-down cleaning protocols to prevent cross-contamination between office areas and sensitive lab zones. We are experienced with badge-access buildings, security protocols, and the documentation requirements that many life sciences tenants mandate from their cleaning vendors.

Technology Companies & Startups

Cambridge's tech companies — from Google's Kendall Square campus to early-stage startups in Central Square — often operate in open-plan offices with collaborative spaces, huddle rooms, standing desks, large monitor setups, and communal kitchens that see heavy daily use. Our cleaning programs for tech offices include thorough breakroom and kitchen sanitization, desk and workstation dusting, monitor and screen-safe cleaning, whiteboard maintenance, and detailed attention to high-touch surfaces like shared keyboards, door handles, and conference room equipment. We understand that many tech companies have flexible schedules, and we adjust our cleaning times to avoid disrupting late-working engineering teams or early-morning standups.

Coworking Spaces & Shared Offices

Cambridge is home to a growing number of coworking spaces — from national operators like WeWork and Regus to independent shared-office communities in Central Square and Kendall Square. Blue Brick provides daily and multi-daily cleaning programs that keep shared surfaces sanitized, common kitchens clean, and restrooms consistently fresh. University-Affiliated Offices

These spaces often have unique scheduling requirements — academic calendars, event cycles, and seasonal fluctuations in occupancy — and need cleaning crews that can adapt to changing needs without missing a beat. What Our Commercial Office Cleaning Includes

Blue Brick's office cleaning programs are built around your facility's specific needs, but most commercial clients in Cambridge rely on a core set of services that we deliver with consistency and precision.

Daily & Weekly Cleaning Programs

  • General Office Cleaning — Dusting of all surfaces, desks, shelving, and windowsills. Vacuuming and mopping of all floor areas. Emptying and relining trash and recycling receptacles throughout the office.
  • Restroom Sanitization — Complete cleaning and disinfection of all restroom surfaces, fixtures, mirrors, and partitions. Restocking of paper products, soap dispensers, and hand sanitizer stations. Restrooms are often the single biggest factor in how employees and visitors perceive a building's cleanliness — we treat them accordingly.
  • Breakroom & Kitchen Cleaning — Wiping down countertops, tables, and appliance exteriors. Cleaning sinks, faucets, and backsplashes. Emptying and sanitizing trash bins. Spot-cleaning cabinet fronts and handles. For offices with heavy kitchen use, we offer daily deep-clean add-ons that include refrigerator cleanouts and microwave interior detailing.
  • High-Touch Surface Disinfection — Door handles, light switches, elevator buttons, stairwell railings, shared printers, and conference room controls are disinfected on every visit. This is not optional — it is a standard part of every Blue Brick cleaning program.

Periodic & Deep Cleaning Services

  • Floor Care — Carpet extraction, hard floor stripping and refinishing, tile and grout deep cleaning, and VCT maintenance. We schedule these services on a quarterly or semi-annual basis depending on traffic levels and flooring type.
  • Window Cleaning — Interior glass cleaning for offices, conference rooms, and glass partitions. We also coordinate exterior window cleaning for ground-level and accessible facades.
  • Upholstery & Fabric Cleaning — Office chairs, lobby seating, and fabric partitions accumulate dust, allergens, and stains over time. Periodic upholstery cleaning extends the life of your furniture and improves indoor air quality.
  • Air Vent & Light Fixture Cleaning — HVAC diffusers, return grilles, and light fixtures collect dust that circulates through the office. We include these in our deep-clean rotations to maintain air quality and keep fixtures looking their best.

Flexible Programs: Every Cambridge office is different. Blue Brick builds custom cleaning schedules — from one night per week to five nights plus weekend service — based on your square footage, headcount, and specific facility needs. No long-term contracts required.
