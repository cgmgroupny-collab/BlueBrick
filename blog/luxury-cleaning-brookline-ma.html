Blue Brick's luxury residential cleaning service in Brookline is not simply cleaning — it is a systematic, detail-level approach to maintaining your home's quality. What White-Glove Cleaning Includes

  • Surface-specific products — marble cleaner for stone, hardwood-safe products for wood floors, stainless steel polish for appliances, silver polish for fixtures
  • Detail cleaning — baseboards hand-wiped, light switch plates cleaned, grout lines scrubbed, cabinet faces polished
  • Luxury bathroom service — fixtures polished to shine, mirrors streak-free, towels folded hotel-style, products organized
  • Kitchen detail — appliance exteriors polished, inside microwave and oven cleaned, cabinet fronts wiped, countertops disinfected
  • Bedroom care — linens changed if requested, under-furniture dusting, closet surfaces dusted
  • Finishing touches — cushions straightened, throws folded, window coverings dusted, fresh air circulation if weather allows

Brookline homes with antique hardwood floors: We use only hardwood-manufacturer-approved cleaning products. We never use steam mops or excessive moisture on original or refinished hardwood — a common mistake that causes cupping and warping.
