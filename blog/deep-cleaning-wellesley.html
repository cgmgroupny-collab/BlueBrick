📋 What Deep Cleaning Includes

Deep cleaning is a comprehensive, top-to-bottom service that goes far beyond what regular maintenance cleaning covers. It targets the buildup, grime, and neglected areas that accumulate over weeks and months of daily living. Here is what Blue Brick's deep cleaning covers in a Wellesley home.

🍳 Kitchen Deep Cleaning

The kitchen is the most demanding room in any deep clean. Our team degreases range hoods, exhaust fans, and backsplash areas. We clean inside and behind appliances, including refrigerators, ovens, dishwashers, and microwaves. All cabinet fronts and handles are wiped down. Countertops are sanitized using products appropriate for the specific stone or surface material. Sink basins and fixtures are descaled and polished. For Wellesley kitchens with luxury appliances like Sub-Zero, Wolf, or Miele units, we follow manufacturer-recommended cleaning approaches to protect warranties and finishes.

🚿 Bathroom Deep Cleaning

Every bathroom is sanitized from ceiling to floor. We scrub tile grout lines, descale shower glass and fixtures, clean inside medicine cabinets, polish mirrors, and disinfect all high-touch surfaces. Tub surrounds, vanity tops, and floor tiles receive focused attention. In Wellesley's master bathrooms, which often feature heated floors, frameless glass enclosures, freestanding soaking tubs, and natural stone tile, we use pH-neutral products that sanitize effectively without etching or dulling premium surfaces.

🛏️ Living Areas and Bedrooms

Dust accumulates in places most people never notice. We clean ceiling fans, light fixtures, crown molding, baseboards, window sills, and door frames throughout the home. Upholstered furniture is deep-vacuumed. Hardwood floors are carefully cleaned using appropriate methods for the finish type. Area rugs are vacuumed on both sides. Built-in shelving, mantels, and entertainment centers are dusted and wiped. In larger Wellesley homes with formal living rooms, libraries, or home offices, we ensure every room receives the same level of detail.

🪣 Floors, Baseboards, and Trim

All hard flooring is mopped or cleaned according to material type. Baseboards and trim are wiped down throughout the entire home, removing the dust and scuff marks that regular cleaning misses. Staircase railings, newel posts, and baluster details are cleaned individually. For homes with extensive hardwood, we use products that clean without leaving residue or affecting the finish.

🪟 Windows, Vents, and Hidden Areas

Interior windows and window tracks are cleaned. All air vents and registers are wiped to remove dust buildup that affects air quality. We clean behind furniture where possible, inside closets upon request, and address laundry rooms, mudrooms, and utility spaces. These often-overlooked areas are exactly where deep cleaning delivers the most noticeable improvement.

Blue Brick's deep cleaning reaches every corner, every surface, and every detail that routine cleaning leaves behind. We clean to a standard that you can see, feel, and trust.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What does a deep cleaning include in Wellesley?

A deep cleaning goes beyond routine maintenance. It includes scrubbing baseboards, detailed kitchen degreasing (behind and under appliances), sanitizing bathrooms top to bottom, cleaning inside cabinets and drawers, washing interior windows, deep-vacuuming upholstery and carpets, dusting ceiling fans and light fixtures, cleaning vents and registers, and addressing all hard-to-reach areas that regular cleaning overlooks.

How often should I schedule a deep cleaning for my Wellesley home?

For most Wellesley luxury homes, we recommend a deep cleaning every three to six months, depending on household size, pets, and lifestyle. Homes with children, pets, or frequent entertaining benefit from quarterly deep cleans. Seasonal deep cleaning is also popular among Wellesley homeowners who want their property refreshed before holidays or after long winters.

How long does a deep cleaning take for a large Wellesley home?

Deep cleaning times vary based on square footage, condition, and scope. A typical 3,000 to 4,000 square foot Wellesley home takes approximately 5 to 8 hours with a two-person crew. Larger estates exceeding 5,000 square feet may require a full day or a larger team. We provide accurate time estimates after an initial assessment.

Do you use products safe for premium finishes and natural stone?

Yes. Blue Brick uses pH-neutral, non-abrasive cleaning products specifically selected for premium materials including marble, granite, quartzite, hardwood, and designer fixtures. We never use generic all-purpose cleaners on high-end surfaces. Each material receives the appropriate product and technique to clean effectively without causing damage.
