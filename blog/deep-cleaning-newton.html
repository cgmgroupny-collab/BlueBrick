📋 What a Professional Deep Clean Includes

A Blue Brick deep clean goes far beyond what most people picture when they hear the phrase "house cleaning." This is a comprehensive, multi-hour service that addresses every room, surface, and hidden area in your Newton home. Here is what our process covers:

🍳 Kitchen Deep Clean

We degrease and sanitize all countertops, backsplashes, and range hoods. Cabinet fronts and handles are wiped down. Appliances are cleaned inside and out -- ovens, microwaves, refrigerators, and dishwashers. We address grout lines in tile flooring and backsplash, clean sink basins and fixtures to a polish, and ensure that every drawer, shelf, and surface is free of residue and buildup. For Newton's luxury kitchens with natural stone countertops, we use pH-neutral cleaners that protect the finish.

🚿 Bathrooms

Every bathroom receives a thorough deep clean: tile scrubbing, grout restoration, glass shower door descaling, fixture polishing, and vanity sanitization. We clean behind toilets, inside medicine cabinets, and in the often-neglected spaces around and underneath bathroom storage. For homes with marble or granite vanities, we use specialized products that clean without etching or dulling the stone.

🛏️ Living Areas and Bedrooms

We dust all surfaces from ceiling to floor -- ceiling fans, light fixtures, crown molding, window frames, blinds, and baseboards. Upholstered furniture is vacuumed, including under cushions. Hardwood and tile floors are cleaned with appropriate products, and carpeted areas receive thorough vacuuming with attention to edges and corners. We wipe down all switches, outlets, and door hardware -- the high-touch surfaces that accumulate grime invisibly over time.

Specialized Areas

For Newton's larger homes, our deep clean extends to areas that standard services typically skip: mudrooms, laundry rooms, home offices, finished basements, and bonus rooms. We clean interior windows, address cobwebs in high corners and stairwells, wipe down banisters and railings, and ensure that even the least-used spaces in your home receive the same level of attention as the primary living areas.

✨ ✨ ✨ Luxury Home Specific: Protecting Premium Materials

Newton's finest homes are filled with materials that require specialized care. Using the wrong product or technique on a premium surface can cause irreversible damage -- and that is a risk Blue Brick eliminates entirely. Our team is trained in the proper handling of every finish material commonly found in luxury Newton properties.

  • Marble and granite surfaces require pH-neutral, non-abrasive cleaners. Acidic products -- including many common household cleaners -- will etch natural stone permanently. We use professional-grade stone-safe products that clean effectively without compromising the finish or sealant.
  • Hardwood floors need moisture-controlled cleaning methods. Excessive water warps wood and damages finishes. We use microfiber systems with controlled dampness and wood-appropriate cleaning solutions that leave floors clean, streak-free, and protected.
  • High ceilings and architectural details -- coffered ceilings, crown molding, exposed beams, and chandeliers -- require extended-reach equipment and a careful hand. Our team is experienced working at height and handling delicate fixtures without damage.
  • Custom cabinetry and millwork with lacquered, painted, or natural wood finishes demand soft-cloth techniques and finish-specific products. We never use abrasive pads or harsh chemicals on cabinetry that took craftsmen weeks to build.
  • Glass and window walls found in many of Newton's contemporary homes receive streak-free cleaning with professional-grade glass solutions, leaving expansive views crystal clear.

📅 Seasonal Deep Cleaning for Newton Homes

New England's four distinct seasons create specific deep cleaning needs that Newton homeowners understand well. At Blue Brick, we tailor our deep cleaning services to the seasonal demands of the greater Boston climate.

Spring Deep Clean

After a long Massachusetts winter, homes accumulate months of indoor living residue -- tracked-in salt and sand, reduced ventilation buildup, and the general staleness that comes from sealed-up living. A spring deep clean opens the season fresh: we address window tracks clogged with winter debris, deep clean kitchens and bathrooms that have seen heavy use through the cold months, and restore every room to a bright, breathable state.

Summer Refresh

Newton summers bring increased foot traffic, open windows, and pollen. A mid-summer deep clean tackles the dust and allergens that accumulate despite regular maintenance, and prepares your home for the entertaining season when Newton families host gatherings, cookouts, and holiday celebrations.

Fall Preparation

As temperatures drop and homes begin to seal up for winter, a fall deep clean ensures that you are not trapping months of accumulated dust, allergens, and grime inside your living space. We pay special attention to HVAC registers, window seals, and the transitional spaces -- mudrooms, entryways, and garages -- that take the brunt of New England's leaf season.

Winter Deep Clean

Holiday hosting, reduced daylight, and increased time indoors make winter an ideal time for a comprehensive deep clean. Whether you are preparing for guests or simply want to start the new year with an immaculate home, our winter deep cleaning service addresses every surface and space that matters.
