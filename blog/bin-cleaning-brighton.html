What's Included in Every Bin Cleaning

Every Blue Brick bin cleaning in Brighton includes the same 7-step process, whether it's a one-time clean or your 12th monthly service. No upsells, no hidden charges, no skipped steps.

200-Degree Pressure Wash

Truck-mounted hot water pressure washer blasts the inside, outside, lid, and wheels of each bin at 200 degrees Fahrenheit -- hot enough to melt greasy buildup and kill bacteria on contact.

EPA-Registered Sanitizer

After pressure washing, every bin is treated with a commercial-grade, EPA-registered sanitizer that kills E. coli, salmonella, listeria, and the bacteria responsible for trash odor. Safe around pets and children.

Deodorizer & Fresh Scent

A final application of commercial deodorizer leaves the bin with a light, clean scent that lasts 3 to 4 weeks. Not perfume -- a neutral clean smell that masks nothing and actually eliminates odor at the source.
