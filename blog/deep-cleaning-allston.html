📅 September 1st and April 1st: Allston's Turnover Seasons

Boston's lease cycle is unlike any other city in the country, and Allston sits at the center of it. September 1st is the single largest moving day in the neighborhood, when thousands of leases expire simultaneously and the streets fill with moving trucks, discarded furniture, and the organized chaos of an entire neighborhood reshuffling. April 1st brings a smaller but still significant wave of turnovers as mid-year leases end and students shift housing for the spring semester.

For landlords and property managers, these dates create an unavoidable crunch. Units must be cleaned, inspected, repaired, and ready for new tenants within a window that can be as short as 24 to 48 hours. A standard cleaning service cannot handle this kind of demand. Deep cleaning during turnover season means stripping a unit down to baseline: removing months or years of accumulated grime from kitchens, sanitizing bathrooms that have seen heavy use, scrubbing floors back to their original condition, and ensuring every cabinet, closet, and corner is spotless before the next tenant walks in.

📋 What a Deep Cleaning Includes

A deep cleaning goes well beyond surface-level tidying. It targets the built-up grime, grease, dust, and residue that accumulates in areas regular cleaning overlooks. Here is what Blue Brick covers during a deep clean in Allston:

🍳 Kitchen

  • Degrease and sanitize stovetop, burners, drip pans, and range hood
  • Clean inside the oven, microwave, and refrigerator (including shelves and drawers)
  • Scrub and disinfect countertops, backsplash, and sink basin
  • Wipe down all cabinet fronts and clean inside cabinets and drawers
  • Clean dishwasher interior and exterior
  • Scrub floor on hands and knees, including grout lines and baseboards
  • Wipe down light fixtures, switch plates, and outlet covers

🚿 Bathrooms

  • Scrub and sanitize toilet (including base, hinges, and behind the tank)
  • Deep clean shower walls, tub, and glass doors to remove soap scum and hard water buildup
  • Descale faucets, showerheads, and drain covers
  • Clean and polish mirrors, vanity, and countertops
  • Scrub tile grout and floor on hands and knees
  • Wipe down exhaust fan, towel bars, and medicine cabinet interior

🛏️ Living Areas and Bedrooms

  • Dust and wipe all surfaces including windowsills, ledges, and shelving
  • Clean window tracks and interior window glass
  • Wipe down baseboards, door frames, and light switches throughout
  • Vacuum and mop all floors, including under furniture and in closets
  • Dust ceiling fans, vents, and light fixtures
  • Clean inside all closets (shelves, rods, and floors)
