Cambridge is unlike any other commercial market in Greater Boston. The concentration of biotech companies in Kendall Square, universities in Harvard Square, and tech startups along Mass Ave creates a diverse range of commercial cleaning needs — from standard office environments to lab-adjacent spaces with specialized requirements.

Blue Brick has cleaned commercial spaces across Cambridge for years. We understand the market: the 6am opening schedules, the hot desk environments, the shared amenity spaces in co-working buildings, and the strict landlord standards in class A office buildings.

Cambridge Commercial Cleaning Specialties

  • Tech and startup offices — hot-desk sanitization, common areas, kitchen and coffee stations, glass walls and conference rooms
  • Lab-adjacent spaces — break rooms, hallways, restrooms, and office areas adjacent to research facilities; we do not enter active lab spaces
  • Retail and restaurant — floor maintenance, restroom cleaning, window cleaning, kitchen support areas
  • Co-working and shared offices — daily desk sanitization, event space reset, printer and tech surface cleaning
  • Medical and dental offices — OSHA-compliant disinfection protocols for Cambridge healthcare providers
