📦 Our Move-In / Move-Out Cleaning Checklist

Every Blue Brick move-in/move-out clean follows a comprehensive checklist designed to meet the standards that landlords, property managers, and new occupants expect.

🍳 Kitchen

  • Clean inside and outside all appliances — oven, refrigerator, dishwasher, microwave, range hood
  • Degrease stovetop, backsplash, and range hood filters
  • Clean inside all cabinets, drawers, and pantry shelves
  • Scrub sink and polish fixtures
  • Clean countertops and backsplash
  • Wipe down light switches, outlet covers, and switchplates
  • Mop and detail floor including edges and corners

🚿 Bathrooms

  • Deep clean and sanitize toilet, including base and behind
  • Scrub shower/tub tile and grout
  • Clean glass shower doors and tracks
  • Polish mirrors and fixtures
  • Clean inside medicine cabinet and vanity
  • Scrub sink and countertop
  • Clean exhaust fan cover
  • Mop floor including behind toilet and under vanity

All Rooms

  • Dust and wipe all surfaces — shelves, windowsills, door frames, baseboards
  • Clean all interior windows and tracks
  • Clean inside all closets — shelves, rods, floors
  • Dust light fixtures, ceiling fans, and vents
  • Remove cobwebs from corners and ceilings
  • Vacuum all carpeted areas and mop all hard floors
  • Clean all door handles, light switches, and outlet covers
  • Spot-clean walls for scuffs and marks

Additional Areas

  • Clean laundry area — inside/around washer and dryer connections
  • Wipe down entry doors, storm doors, and hardware
  • Clean interior side of all windows
  • Vacuum and clean any storage spaces

📋 Move-Out vs. Move-In: What is the Difference?

Move-out cleaning focuses on restoring the property to the condition it was in at the start of the lease or prior to the sale. The goal is to remove all traces of the previous occupant's use, satisfy landlord or buyer inspection requirements, and protect the tenant's or seller's financial interests.

Move-in cleaning focuses on ensuring the property is sanitized, fresh, and genuinely ready for the new occupant. Even in a recently cleaned property, new residents often want a thorough cleaning on their own terms before unpacking and settling in.

Blue Brick handles both. Many clients book us for the move-out clean at their old address and the move-in clean at their new one.
